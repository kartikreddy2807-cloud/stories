Crete was our first stop. As soon as we arrived, I noticed cats everywhere—on steps, under chairs at cafés, and sleeping in the shade like they owned the island.

We spent a lot of time eating. I tried dishes like moussaka, souvlaki, Greek salad, tzatziki, and gyros, and everything came with plenty of bread and feta cheese.[web:5][web:23][web:26]

When we were not eating, we were at the beach. The water was clear and blue, and the sunsets made the whole sky glow.
