Spontaneous Summer in Europe

From Crete to Budapest to Amsterdam

Home Crete, Greece Budapest & Beyond Amsterdam

It started with a half-serious joke over coffee: “What if we just went to Europe this summer?” A few weeks, too many tabs, and one impulsive ticket purchase later, I was rolling a suitcase across an airport floor, headed into a trip that hadn’t been planned so much as launched.

The rough outline was simple: chase the sun on the island of Crete in Greece, wander through the history and hills of Budapest in Hungary, and end in the canals and bikes of Amsterdam in the Netherlands. No rigid itinerary, just a string of flights, a few addresses in my notes app, and a lot of curiosity.

What followed was a blur of sea salt and street food, late-night conversations with family and strangers, and the kind of small moments that only happen when you leave a little room for chaos in your plans. This site is a hyperlink narrative of that summer: click through each city to follow the story and the photos that went with it.
