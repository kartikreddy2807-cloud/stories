Crete was our first stop. As soon as we arrived, I noticed cats everywhere. On steps, under chairs at cafés, and sleeping any and everywhere as if they owned the island.

We spent a lot of time eating. I tried dishes like moussaka, souvlaki, and of course Gyros. It was all amazing

When we were not eating, we were at the beach. Crete had some of the clearest water I've ever seen
